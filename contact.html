Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for a job?

Simply browse jobs, click on a position, and hit the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to create an account first.

Is TrueJobs really free?

Yes! Creating an account and applying for jobs is completely free for job seekers.

How are employers verified?

We verify business licenses, contact information, and company registration before approving any employer.

What if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
