Our Story

TrueJobs was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a bridge between talented professionals and trusted employers across the globe. What started as a small platform has now grown into a thriving community of over 50,000 job seekers and 1,200 companies.

We recognized that the job search process was often frustrating, opaque, and filled with uncertainty. Many professionals struggled to find legitimate opportunities, while employers found it challenging to connect with qualified candidates. TrueJobs was built to solve these exact problems.

Today, we're proud to have facilitated over 15,000 successful job placements across 45+ countries. Our commitment to verification, transparency, and user experience has made us a trusted name in the employment industry.
